Who we are?

The Tucumã Project was found by the “ Environment Preservation” theme and had your start at the Pre-TCC, idealized by the Infonet’s Second grade students from ETEC de Poá, in 2019.

We look for means of searching, entertainment, educacional games, and mainly awareness, alert and teach people about the environment and Amazônia, that is present in all sectors of our lives and encompass a different care.

What do we preserv?

The Amazonian forest is almost a legend: A large green world of rivers and forests, where the top of the giant trees hide the damp birth, reprodution and death of more than one third of species that exists on Earth.

With about 45 thousands species of plants and animals, the Amazonian forest becomes one of the most important forests in the world, like it was said before, and with that it becomes necessary the preservation and the awareness about this place, for it not to cause damage on the environment, this is what we are loooking for.
